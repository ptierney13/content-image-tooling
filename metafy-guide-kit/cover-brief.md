# Cover Brief

Metafy currently recommends a `1920x1080` cover image in a `16:9` ratio.

## Goal

Make the guide feel sharp, deliberate, and competitive at a glance.

## Best cover direction

- Put the deck’s champion or defining visual at the center.
- Use strong contrast and clean lighting.
- Keep the image readable even when cropped small.
- Avoid placing important text on the image.

## Visual themes that usually work

- Tournament-energy: bold lighting, sharp contrast, competitive feel
- Rogue-deck mystery: darker framing, one bright focal point, unusual card glow
- Technical mastery: clean composition, confident pose, crisp color blocking

## Prompt starter for an artist or image tool

"Create a competitive trading card game guide cover for a Riftbound deck guide. Focus on [champion/archetype], with dramatic lighting, strong contrast, premium editorial composition, and a polished esports feel. The image should read clearly at thumbnail size, avoid embedded text, and leave enough safe framing so cropping does not hide the focal subject."

## File checklist

- 1920x1080
- No important text baked in
- Strong focal subject
- Looks good at thumbnail size
- Matches the deck identity
