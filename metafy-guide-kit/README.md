# Riftbound Metafy Guide Kit

This starter pack is built to help you do two jobs at once:

1. Publish a clear, valuable Riftbound guide on Metafy.
2. Use that guide to create attention for your deck, your testing, and your future plans.

## Files

- `deck-intake.md`: Fill this out first so the rest of the writing goes faster.
- `metafy-guide-template.md`: Main guide draft structure.
- `metafy-listing-copy.md`: Title, short description, pricing angle, and CTA copy.
- `publicity-launch-plan.md`: How to launch the guide so people actually see it.
- `deck-cheat-sheet-template.md`: Fast-reference page you can share or include in the guide.
- `cover-brief.md`: Cover image direction for a cleaner Metafy listing.

## Recommended approach

If your main goal is publicity, not immediate revenue, lead with one of these:

- Free guide with a strong deck identity and regular updates.
- Low-ticket guide ($5-$10) if you already have some trust or results.

That gives more players a reason to try your list, share it, and talk about it.

## Suggested publish flow

1. Fill out `deck-intake.md`.
2. Draft the guide in `metafy-guide-template.md`.
3. Pull your listing title and description from `metafy-listing-copy.md`.
4. Publish the guide on Metafy.
5. Use `publicity-launch-plan.md` for your announcement posts and week-one follow-up.

## Notes on Metafy

Based on Metafy Help Center guidance and current Riftbound guide listings checked on March 29, 2026:

- Guides can be free, one-time purchase, or subscriber-only.
- Guides can include text, images, videos, quotes, dividers, Google Sheets, and links.
- Cover images should be 1920x1080 at 16:9.
- Clear titles, concrete promises, and regular updates are common patterns among visible Riftbound guides.
